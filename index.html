Distribution of winter clothing

Soon it will rain again. The many internally displaced people need a change of clothes. We call this relief effort “wardrobe,” even though it doesn’t quite fit. The families who need help in winter 2019/20 have nothing left, including a wardrobe. They are refugees in their own country, i.e. internal refugees. They are mostly single women, old people, children and people with disabilities.
